LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Place your Helping Hand Systems logo in this directory as:

  hhs-logo.png

The logo should be:
- PNG format (with transparent background preferred)
- High resolution (at least 400x400px recommended)
- Will display at approximately 128px height on the form

Current expected path: /public/assets/hhs-logo.png

If you rename the logo file, update the path in:
  src/App.jsx (line 11)
